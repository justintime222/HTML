Illegally downloading movies

The selected issue for VCE Computing, Unit 1, Outcome 3 is in the entertainment field of illegal downloading of media films.

The main stakeholders for this issue are those who produced the films (those in the films credits), all users who watch the films (both who legally and illegally watch it), owners of sites and those who illegally share it.

The issue will result in those who produced the films having a decreased income, a consequence of this is that films prices could be increased. Exploring this issue will show how people are impacted, the reasons for this and possible solutions.
