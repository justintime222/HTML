Analysis of Movie Piracy

Interview

 

The people that were interviewed all would have preferred to use a file download as a legal option and used legal sources daily, although none really wanted to use legal options, as highlighted by K.D. saying it would take 'A gun at my head' to make him use legal options only.

(Dekker, K. & Neil, May (2016). Movie Torrenting. Berwick Grammar School.)

Secondary data

Loss of revenue is of concern because it is unstoppable, it can cause lower salaries for lower-end workers, but is very unlikely to directly impact on actors and directors.
