Our View

We acknowledge that there may be some damage done to the film industry by piracy, but we believe that it is nowhere near as bad as it is made to seem, our contention is that if the movie industry puts out a figure comparing how much they make compared to how much is lost we may believe them. For all we know, the few billions of dollars they say are done in damages, do they make in hundreds of billions? If so then how exactly does a few percent of their income pose such a threat?
